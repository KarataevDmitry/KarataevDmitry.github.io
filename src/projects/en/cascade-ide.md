Cascade IDE applies a cockpit attention model (PFD/MFD/EICAS) to software work: editor-first focus, deliberate secondary zones, and explicit alerting policy.

| Capability | Why it matters | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Attention zones (PFD/MFD/EICAS) | Controls visual priority and reduces context-switch tax | In active development |
| MCP-first agent orchestration | Keeps toolchain evidence in one observable loop | In active development |
| Workspace navigation presets | Stable related-file affordances for large multi-file work | Documented + evolving |
| Safety and pre-flight patterns | Clarification, review-before-apply, and predictable operations | ADR-backed direction |
